# Finance-Agent-Standard (#63)

## Tagline
The open-source standard for autonomous financial analysis.

## What It Does
Providing a highly regulated suite of SKILL.md files, this tool teaches an agent how to pull SEC filings, calculate complex DCF models, and generate unbiased equity research reports, complete with mathematically verified citations.

## Inspired By
Bloomberg Terminal, OpenBB, RAG + FinTech

## Viral Potential
Directly disrupts wildly expensive financial data monopolies. AI Wall Street Analyst narrative is catnip for social media. Generates highly actionable, high-value output.

## Unique Defensible Moat
A proprietary retrieval engine cross-references LLM outputs against live SEC Edgar databases to mathematically guarantee zero hallucination in financial figures, establishing absolute trust.

/data-fetcher, /skills, MIT License, AAPL analysis demo
